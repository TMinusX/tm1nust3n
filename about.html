About T-X
T-X (T minus Ten) is a launch pad protocol designed to favour the community by providing access to the best new projects at the TEN network while also providing long-term value for TMX token holders.
Vetting new projects before they launch is a central part of getting live at T-X. This means all projects have gone through a process to prove financial stability, viability of the project, a comprehensive team background check, and more. As a part of the TEN network, we aim for a high standard and financial sustainability.
TEN partnership
As the official launchpad at the TEN network, we secure an inflow of new projects that launch at TEN. Providing the community with native tokens is important to growing the ecosystem by engaging new users.
